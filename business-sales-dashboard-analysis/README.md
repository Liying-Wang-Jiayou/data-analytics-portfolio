# Business Sales Dashboard & Customer Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview
This project presents a business intelligence dashboard built using Excel to analyze retail sales and customer purchasing behavior. The dashboard was developed based on real-world sales data exported from a POS system and manually cleaned and structured for analysis.

The goal of this project was to support data-driven decision making in sales strategy, marketing performance, and staff training.

## Data Source
- Retail sales data exported from POS (Point-of-Sale) system
- Data manually cleaned, structured, and transformed using Excel
- Includes transaction-level data across customers, products, categories, promotions, and sales staff

## Tools & Techniques
- Excel Pivot Tables
- Data Cleaning and Transformation
- Dashboard Design and Layout
- Excel Formulas and Functions
- Data Visualization (Bar Charts, Line Charts, Pie Charts)
- Trend and Performance Analysis

## Key Analysis Areas

### Customer Behavior Analysis
- Customer segmentation and purchasing patterns
- Shopping frequency and behavior across different customer groups

### Sales Performance Analysis
- Monthly, weekly, and yearly sales trends
- Popular products and top-performing categories
- Top 10 brands by category

### Promotion & Event Analysis
- Sales performance during different promotional events
- Comparison of the same event across different years
- Comparison of multiple events within a year

### Category & Product Insights
- Sales comparison across categories
- Identification of high-performing and low-performing products
- Trend analysis for key product groups

### Staff Performance Analysis
- Sales performance by staff across different categories
- Identification of strengths and weaknesses by category
- Insights used to support targeted staff training

## Key Impact
- Enabled data-driven decision making for business and marketing strategy
- Provided clear visualization of customer and sales trends
- Supported staff training through performance insights
- Improved understanding of product and category performance
